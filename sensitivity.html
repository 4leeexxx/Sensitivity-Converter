Switching games but want to keep the same mouse feel? Our free sensitivity converter translates your sensitivity across all major FPS titles — Valorant, CS2 (Counter-Strike 2), Fortnite, Apex Legends, Overwatch 2, Rainbow Six Siege, Warzone, Rust, Escape from Tarkov, and more.

What Is eDPI?

eDPI (effective DPI) = your mouse DPI × your in-game sensitivity. It's the true measure of how fast your crosshair moves. A Valorant player at 800 DPI × 0.32 sens has an eDPI of 256. Most pro players sit between 200–400 eDPI. Knowing your eDPI lets you match your feel perfectly across any game.

What Is cm/360°?

cm/360° is how many centimetres you need to move your mouse to complete a full 360° rotation in-game. This is the most universal way to compare sensitivities across games. Most FPS pros use between 20–50 cm/360°.
